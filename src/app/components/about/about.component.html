About the Photographer

Thanks for taking the time to come visit! Jess here. Mom. Friend. Nomad. Body positivity activist. Animal lover. Creator behind Project: Self Love. Co creator of three amazing little humans. I have been a portrait photographer for ten years and have worked and traveled all over the country but have settled down in the Nashville area. I began my photography education as a means to capturing my own family and life but as a student of humanity, I quickly fell in love with being present for and documenting other people's lives, along with all the big and little events along the way. From births, to weddings, babies and families, I'm here to help you freeze time. My photography style is collaborative and documentary and I'm interested in capturing you and your loved ones exactly as you are.
